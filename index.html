What is this?

I made this website quite a while back, as sort of a online journal to keep me sane. As expected, I've so far made only a singular entry, but I now wish to revive this project. Both to hone my rudimentary html skills, and to serve as an outlet for me.

Why Wednesday?

Wednesday is quite a special day for me, being the day of the shitstorm that inspired me to make this website, the last day I had irl with my gf, the day I probably look forward the most in the week, and more. Also, I usually post here every Wednesday, look out for that!
